Global Impact Academy Garden Club

What is the Garden Club?

The GIA Garden Club is a student run and led club at Global Impact Acadmey in Fairburn, Ga. We are on a mission to grow our STEM skills and make our school a healthier place.

The school garden was started by out GIA Chief Science Officers in March 2022. Today, feel free to go check out our plants now located in the green field behind the cafeteria.
We have lavender, 2 varieties of kale, pansies, and collards currently planted for the winter.

What are your goals for this school year?

This school year, we hope to:

  1. Expand the Garden
    • Build more garden boxes
    • Plant seasonal plants
  2. Increase garden care efficiency
  3. Spread school's knowledge of the garden
