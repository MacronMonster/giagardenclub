Spring 2022

These are pictures from the day the garden was created!

Winter 2022

These pictures are from our winter gardening day for the 2022-23 school year.
We moved the garden to be behind the cafeteria so that it is more accessible to our stakeholders. We planted lavendar, rosemary, pansies, purple kale, ornamental kale, and collards.
